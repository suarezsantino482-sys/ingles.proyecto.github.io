Actividad 5: QA Simulation - Script

Client: Good morning, team. Thank you for this interview. I need to hire IT consultants for my company. To start, have you ever managed professional documents for international clients?

Consultant 1 (Tupac): Good morning! Yes, we have. Our team has designed standard layout templates for over three years. However, we have never delivered a traditional or boring CV. Using SCAMPER, we changed our CV into a dynamic portfolio with real school projects.

Client: Excellent. My company has problems with teamwork and losing files. Have you ever faced these communication problems?

Consultant 3 (Ezequiel): Yes, we know that problem. Our team has managed strict version control workflows since we entered this technical school. Personally, I have never lost important data because our system completely eliminates mistakes.

Client: Great. Now let’s talk about internet connection. Our rural office in Salta has a terrible connection. Have you ever audited regional ISPs under difficult conditions?

Consultant 2 (Santino): Yes, we have! We just finished a big connectivity audit in Salta. We have never recommended Dial-up or ADSL for a business because they are completely obsolete and have a very high latency of 400 milliseconds.

Client: Good point. What technology do you suggest for our isolated offices in the mountains?

Consultant 4 (Ariel): For remote areas, we have never deployed cables because it is too expensive. Instead, Starlink is authorized in Argentina since 2024. It is perfect for the Puna salteña because it offers high bandwidth and low latency.

Client: Perfect. One last question about your school: Have you ever implemented the newest Wi-Fi 7 standard?

Consultant 5 (Fabrizio): To be honest, our school has not deployed Wi-Fi 7 in the laboratory yet. But we have just finished configuring new routing matrices to handle intensive bandwidth demands.

Client: Amazing! Your technical vocabulary and preparation are excellent. Thank you very much, team.

All Team (Together): Thank you for your time! We look forward to working with you.
